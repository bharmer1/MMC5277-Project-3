Serving The Community Since 2000.

S & L Auto Sales is a family-run used car dealership where our mission is to earn customers for life. We specialize in selling used cars and trucks to people living in South Florida and the surrounding area. Our dealership is located in Hollywood, FL at 2845 N. Federal Highway. We pride ourselves on the service we provide to our customers, along with providing afforable options. While all of our vehicles are sold as-is, without any warranty, we will work with customers on an individual basis to remedy any issues you may have. At S & L Auto Sales, we value our customers and their satisfaction. We know we are doing things right when our customers keep coming back to us for their transportation needs and when they refer their friends and family to us.
